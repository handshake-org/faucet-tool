HNS Address Generator

This tool generates the first receiving address, in the first account of a BIP44 HD wallet and the 24-word BIP39 mnemonic phrase which was used to seed the wallet. It is important that you keep this seed phrase private. It is the only way to access your HNS coins. Do not provide your mnemonic seed phrase to anyone that should not have access to the contents of the wallet.
